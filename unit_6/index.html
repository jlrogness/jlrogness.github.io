About me

I like clean lines, great images and white space. Organizing data into a visual form is my specialty. Whether it's a graphic, brochure, magazine spread or social media image, I like the challenge of creating an easy-to-understand visual. My experience in the newspaper industry has taught me to multi-task under tight deadlines and how to work within a team as well as independently. I am familiar with printing processes and have worked on print products from conception to print.

A few things you may not know about me ... I like to bake anything sweet, including cupcakes, brownies and cheesecakes. I love to travel, with my favorite cities being Savannah, Ga. and London. During the summer months, I can be found in nature camping, hiking or on the lake in an inflatable kayak. One of my biggest accomplishments is finishing the cycling event RAGBRAI, not once but twice for a total of over 800 miles across hilly (who knew) Iowa. I'll be adding a third this summer.
